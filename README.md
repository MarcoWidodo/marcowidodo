# Marco Widodo
**International Development Policy | Stanford University**
*Bridging political economy and data science to navigate institutional friction in the Global South.*

### 🚀 Featured Project
**[Asian Barometer Survey Interactive Explorer](https://abs-explorer-sigma.vercel.app)**
A custom React-based platform for exploring public opinion and democratic trends across Asia. Built with Vite, Tailwind, and Recharts to visualize complex longitudinal survey data across multiple waves.

### 🔬 Experience & Impact
* **Global South Development (World Bank DIME):** Engineered reproducible R pipelines to harmonize cross-country agricultural datasets in Sub-Saharan Africa, focusing on the technical scalability of development policy.
* **Political Economy & Institutional Analysis (CDDRL):** Principal Investigator for a 4x4 mixed factorial survey experiment in Indonesia, analyzing how institutional perceptions shape responses to democratic transgression.
* **Asian Policy & Governance (SNAPL):** Applied machine learning for text analysis to parliamentary discourse and state reports on nationalism and multiculturalism.
* **Leadership (KIDA):** Co-President of the King International Development Association at Stanford.

### 🛠 Technical

* **Methods:** Survey Design, NLP / Text-as-Data, Institutional Mapping, Data Visualization
* **Stack:** React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Git, R Markdown
